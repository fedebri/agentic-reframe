# 4. Secondary Research

## Purpose

Establish what is already true, already tried, structurally limiting, or known
from external sources before ideation begins.

## Inputs

- Brief and supporting documents
- User-supplied research notes in `human_input/secondary_research/`
- Prior graph claims that require external validation

## Tasks

1. Extract facts, constraints, prior attempts, and contextual conditions from
   available documents.
2. Distinguish verified facts from assumptions or unvalidated claims.
3. Identify structural limits such as funding, infrastructure, regulation,
   policy, staffing, geography, or prior failed attempts.
4. Mark claims that need human or external evidence.
5. Summarize research gaps that could change the challenge frame.

## Expected Outcome

- Landscape facts.
- Constraint facts.
- Prior-attempt notes.
- Research gaps and validation questions.
- Claims that should block or caution later ideation.

## Provenance Requirements

Every fact must cite a source document or human-provided research note. Do not
upgrade agentic inference to fact without evidence.
