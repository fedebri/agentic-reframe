# 2. Align Impact Goals

## Purpose

Clarify the change the project is trying to create, separating near-term
learning goals from longer-term impact goals.

## Inputs

- Current framed challenge candidates
- `docs/brief.md`
- Relevant stakeholder or human-input evidence

## Tasks

1. Extract explicit and implied impact goals from the brief.
2. Separate outputs, outcomes, and long-term impact.
3. Identify whose definition of success is represented or missing.
4. Surface goal conflicts, trade-offs, and measurement risks.
5. Propose success signals suitable for the discovery phase.

## Expected Outcome

- Near-term and long-term impact goals.
- Success signals that can be validated later.
- Goal conflicts or unresolved stakeholder trade-offs.
- Questions for human review where success is ambiguous.

## Provenance Requirements

Record each goal as a claim with evidence source, stakeholder perspective,
confidence, and validation status. Connect goal conflicts with `contradicts` or
`accepted_tension` edges.
