# 6. Resource Flow

## Purpose

Map critical resource flows among stakeholders. Money is the default starting
point, but the model may also track time, labor, care, infrastructure,
attention, trust, data, and other scarce resources.

## Inputs

- Stakeholder map
- Human-input resource-flow maps in `human_input/resource_flow/`
- Interview notes, field observations, or brief evidence about resource use

## Tasks

1. Identify incoming and outgoing resources for each relevant stakeholder.
2. Record resource type, frequency, dependency, and perceived value.
3. Identify bottlenecks, leakage, scarcity, and timing mismatches.
4. Distinguish observed flows from inferred flows.
5. Generate a resource-flow capture template if human evidence is absent.

## Expected Outcome

- Resource-flow map by stakeholder.
- Bottleneck and dependency claims.
- Timing or frequency notes.
- Missing-evidence template when needed.

## Provenance Requirements

Every flow must include source, resource type, direction, stakeholder endpoint,
confidence, and validation status. Inferred flows must remain hypotheses until
validated.
